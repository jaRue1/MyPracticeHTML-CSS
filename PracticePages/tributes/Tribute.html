Mike Tyson

Career Accomplishments

  • 1985 March 6th Win's First Pro Fight against Hector Mercedes
  • 1985 December 27th won his 15th fight against Mark Young ( KO streak of 15)
  • 1986 Febuary 16th, Beats Jesse Ferguson by way of TKO
  • 1986 May 3rd Wins by unanimous decision for the first time ending his KO streak of 18
  • 1986 November 22nd Became the youngest boxer to win the WBC heavyweight title, at 20 years, four months, and 22 days old
  • 1987 March 7th defeats James Smith for the WBA title
  • 1987 August 1st Defeats Tony Tucker for the IBF title
  • 1987 Unifies All three titles in the Heavy Weight Divsion
  • 1988 Succesfully Defends Unified titles (3 Title defences)
  • 1989 Succesfully Defends Unified titles (2 Title defences)
  • 1990 Feburary 11th Suffers first loss to James Douglas
  • 1996 March 3rd Regains WBC Title Over Frank Bruno
  • 2005 June 11th Fought his last pro fight after which he retired
