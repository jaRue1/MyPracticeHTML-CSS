Here's a time line of Mahatma Gandhi

  • 1869-Born in Gujarat, India
  • 1888-Gandhi travels to London to train as a barrister
  • 1891-Gandhi returns to India after passing the Bar
  • 1894-Gandhi founded the Natal Indian Congress
  • 1906-Gandhi introduced Non-Violent Protest Philosophy of Satyagraha
  • 1930-Gandhi concludes Satyagraha, Salt March
  • 1932-Gandhi starts a six-day fast to protest separate elections for untouchables
  • 1934-Gandhi retires from Politics to focus on village economics
  • 1948-Gandhi announces Fast to end Hindu/Muslim violence
  • 1948-Gandhi is assasinated by Nathuram Godse

Mahatma Gandhi was a true leader of democracy and human rights.
